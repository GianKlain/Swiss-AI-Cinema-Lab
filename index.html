Swiss AI Cinema Laboratory

Artistic Research Initiative 2025-2027

Exploring the creative frontier between human and machine filmmaking. A two-year collaborative experiment bringing together Zurich's film industry to investigate AI as a new artistic medium.

Explore the Program Join the Initiative

Transforming Fear into Creative Agency

Rather than viewing AI as a threat to traditional cinema, we embrace it as a new creative medium requiring critical exploration and artistic research.

Artistic Research

Creating 15-20 experimental prototypes exploring non-linear narratives, generative aesthetics, and human-AI co-creation. This isn't about efficiency — it's about discovering new forms of cinematic expression.

Open Knowledge

All tools, methodologies, and discoveries will be open-sourced and shared with the global creative community. We're building alternatives to Silicon Valley's commercial AI narrative.

Community Building

Connecting 4-5 production companies, partnering with ZHdK, ETH, and international film festivals. Creating a sustainable network for ongoing artistic exploration.

Two-Year Journey

A carefully structured exploration from foundation to international dissemination.

Phase 1: Foundation

Months 1-6 (March - August 2025)

Infrastructure setup with open-source principles. Initial workshops on "AI as Creative Partner" and "Algorithmic Aesthetics". First experimental prototypes begin.

Phase 2: Exploration

Months 7-18 (September 2025 - August 2026)

Deep dive into artistic experimentation. 10-15 projects in production. Public roundtables with international guests. First exhibition of works.

Phase 3: Dissemination

Months 19-24 (September 2026 - February 2027)

International symposium "Cinema After Algorithms". Open-source toolkit release. Final exhibition and sustainability planning for continued operations.
